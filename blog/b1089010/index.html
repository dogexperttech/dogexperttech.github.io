What happens if you delete Windows System32?

August 20, 2025 | By Dog Expert Tech

⚠️ WARNING: DO NOT DO THIS! Deleting the Windows System32 folder will render your system unusable and may cause permanent data loss.

System32 contains core Windows files, drivers, and services. If you remove this folder, your system will not boot or will crash immediately. Usually, the only way out is to perform a clean reinstall of Windows.

This post and the video below are for educational purposes only. Never try to delete System32 on a real computer.
